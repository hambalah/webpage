I'm a data analyst based in Singapore, working at the intersection of financial markets and data infrastructure. My work spans building investment dashboards, automating reporting pipelines, and making sense of messy market data - across firms like St. James's Place, JPMorgan, and Caladan.

My background in Information Systems (Business Analytics) from SMU gave me the technical foundation, but it's the time spent inside trading desks and investment operations that shaped how I think about data — not just as output, but as a decision-making input that has to be accurate, timely, and trusted.

Outside of work, I run a home café called Coffee by the Porch — featured in Channel News Asia and The Straits Times — and I'm currently preparing for the CFA Level 1 exam in November 2026. I find the overlap between finance theory and day-to-day market data work genuinely interesting.
